Module 1

Module 1: Introduction & Digital Devices Definition Digital literacy is the ability to use digital devices, the internet, and technology effectively and safely.
Importance - Helps in education and online learning - Improves communication - Saves time and effort - Provides job opportunities
Digital Devices - Computer - Smartphone - Tablet
Basic Skills - Typing - Using apps and browsers - Searching for information
Conclusion
Digital literacy is essential for everyone in today’s digital world.

Module 2

Email is used to send messages online.Module 2: Internet & Communication
Internet The internet is a global network connecting millions of computers.
Uses - Searching for information - Watching videos - Online classes - Social networking
Web Browser Software used to access the internet: - Chrome - Edge - Firefox
Email Electronic mail used for communication. Structure: - To - Subject - Message - Attachment
Conclusion The internet and email make communication fast and easy.

Module 3

Module 3: Social Media & Digital Platforms
Social Media Online platforms for connecting and sharing content.
Examples - Instagram - Facebook - WhatsApp
Uses - Communication - Sharing media - News updates
Advantages - Connect globally - Easy interaction
Disadvantages - Addiction - Fake news
Conclusion: Use social media wisely and responsibly.

Module 4

Module 4: Cyber Safety & Security
Cyber Safety Protection from online threats.
Common Threats - Hacking - Phishing - Malware
Safety Tips - Use strong passwords - Do not share OTP - Avoid unknown links
Password Rules - Use letters and numbers - Minimum 6 to 8 characters
Conclusion Cyber safety is necessary to stay protected online.

Module 5

Module 5: Digital Ethics & Future Skills
Digital Ethics Rules for responsible internet use. Good Practices - Respect others - Avoid cyberbullying - Do not spread fake news
Fake News False information spread online.
Future Skills - Coding - AI tools - Digital marketing
Career Scope - Software Developer - Data Analyst
Conclusion Digital literacy builds a strong future and responsible digital citizens.
